·         Procedure – Making a Grilled Cheese Sandwich

o   Preheat skillet over medium heat.

o   Generously butter one side of a slice of bread.

o   Place bread butter-side-down onto skillet and add 1 slice of cheese.

o   Butter a second slice of bread on one side and place butter-side-up on top of sandwich.

o   Grill until lightly browned and flip over.

o   Continue grilling until cheese is melted.
